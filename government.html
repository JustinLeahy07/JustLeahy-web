Profiles in Courage by JFK


The Profiles in Courage Essay Contest, inspired by John F. Kennedy's book of the same name, invites students to demonstrate their understanding of political courage through an original essay. Participants are required to explore the concept as defined by Kennedy, applying it to a contemporary context. The theme involves describing and analyzing an act of political courage by a U.S. elected official since 1917. The contest encourages originality, advising against common topics, and aims to celebrate historical and contemporary instances of political courage while fostering critical thinking. My participation in the Profiles in Courage Essay Contest consists of my research and reports of Minnesotan Political Figures.
